Built for a SaaS content team publishing 3× per week. Writers draft in Notion, apply a "publish" tag, and the rest happens automatically. Claude generates platform-native rewrites (long-form LinkedIn, punchy tweet thread, digest-style email), and Buffer queues them for optimal send times based on historical engagement data.
